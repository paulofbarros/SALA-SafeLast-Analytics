## Overview
SALA is a proprietary predictive framework designed to quantify and mitigate ergonomic risks in the Norwegian transport sector. By integrating real-time environmental variables with human fatigue indicators, the system transforms occupational safety from reactive to predictive intelligence.

The project aligns with YSK (Statens vegvesen) professional competence standards and aims to provide data-driven insights for NAV, transport unions (YTF/NTF), and fleet managers to reduce long-term sickness claims and protect driver health.

## The Core Algorithm
The system calculates the Ergonomic Risk Index ($R_i$) by cross-referencing surface friction factors with physical load and duty time.

### Mathematical Framework:
$R_i = (S_f \cdot L_w) + (F_i \cdot T_h)$

- Sf (Surface Factor): Dynamic coefficients for Snow (1.5), Slush (1.7), and Mud (1.8).
- Lw (Load Weight): Total tonnage being handled.
- Fi (Fatigue Index): Cumulative metabolic cost based on active duty.
- Th (Time/Hours): Duration of continuous physical intervention.
