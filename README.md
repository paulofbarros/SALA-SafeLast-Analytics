## 🚛 The Vision
SALA is a specialized Analytics solution designed to reduce workplace injuries and operational costs in the logistikk og transport industry. By crossing real-time telemetry with biomechanical load data and weather conditions, we provide actionable intelligence to protect the industry's most valuable asset: the driver.

## ⚖️ Legal & Scientific Foundation
Our algorithm is built on the requirements of the **Norwegian Working Environment Act (Arbeidsmiljøloven § 4-4)**, which mandates that work must be arranged to avoid unhealthy physical strain on employees.

* **Target:** 180,000+ professional drivers in Norway.
* **Focus:** Prevention of long-term musculoskeletal injuries (HMS) and fatigue-related accidents.

## 🚀 Key Features
* **Real-time Risk Index:** Live monitoring of fatigue and physical load.
* **Weather-Adaptive Intelligence:** Integration with MET Norway to adjust safety parameters based on road friction (Snow/Ice).
* **Management Dashboard:** A strategic overview for companies to monitor fleet health and prevent "sykemelding" (sick leave).
* **Privacy First:** Fully GDPR compliant and designed for the Norwegian labor market.

## 🛠 Tech Stack
* **Backend:** Python (Serverless Architecture)
* **Real-time Data:** Firebase & WebSockets
* **Frontend:** Modern JS/CSS Ecosystem
* **External APIs:** MET Norway, Google Maps Platform

## 📊 Project Status
We are currently in the **Architecture Design Phase**. The next milestone is the implementation of a **Proof of Concept (PoC)** with transport partners in the Larvik/Oslo corridor.
